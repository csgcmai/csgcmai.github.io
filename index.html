Short Bio

I am a Senior Computer Vision Algorithm Engineer with Joyy Inc., Guangzhou. My research interests are pattern recognition, biometric recognition and security, computer vision and deep learning.

Before joining Joyy, I was a Staff Researcher with Lenovo Machine Intelligence Center, Hong Kong SAR. I recieved the Ph.D degree in computer science from Hong Kong Baptist University in 2018, and the B.Eng degree in computer science and technology from South China University of Technology, in 2013.
