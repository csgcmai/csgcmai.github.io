Short Bio

My name is Guangcan Mai, currently a Computer Vision Researcher with Lenovo Machine Intelligence Center, Hong Kong SAR. My research interests are pattern recognition, biometric recognition and security, computer vision and deep learning.

Before that, I recieved the Ph.D degree in computer science from Hong Kong Baptist University in 2018, and the B.Eng degree in computer science and technology from South China University of Technology, in 2013. I was a visiting scholar at Michigan State University.
